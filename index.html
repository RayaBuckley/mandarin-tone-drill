How it scales later
This version is already organized around initials, finals, and tones in the data model. That makes it straightforward to grow into a larger generated deck later, while keeping the current experience simple enough for daily use.
Update behavior
When a new deployment is available, the app checks for a newer service worker and shows a reload prompt. That should let you update without manually clearing Safari data most of the time.
