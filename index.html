Why these characters
The practice set uses Hanzi that usually map cleanly to the intended tone when spoken by a Chinese voice: 妈/麻/马/骂, 八/拔/把/爸, 薛/学/雪/血, 诗/时/使/是, 知/直/指/至, 锅/国/果/过, 妮/尼/你/逆, and similar sets.
